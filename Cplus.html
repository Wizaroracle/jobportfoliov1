GUESS IT


"Guess It Right" is an engaging word guessing game crafted with C++. The game puts your vocabulary to the test with a simple yet addictive challenge: guess the word! The mechanics are straightforward, making it accessible for players of all levels.

Gameplay:

-Word Guessing: The core gameplay revolves around guessing a secret word. Players are presented with blanks representing each letter of the word, and they must input their guesses one letter at a time.

-Limited Attempts: To add a level of challenge, players have a limited number of attempts to guess the complete word. Each incorrect guess brings them closer to the edge of suspense.

-Feedback System: The game provides instant feedback on each guess. Correctly guessed letters are revealed in the word, while incorrect guesses are accounted for, creating a dynamic and interactive experience.

Score Tracking: Players earn points for correct guesses, and the game can keep track of their scores over multiple rounds. This encourages friendly competition and replayability.
